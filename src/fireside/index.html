Fireside Gathering

Our next Fireside Gathering will be AHL Fireside #1 on Saturday January 6 in Spelletjesafe 2 Klaveren. It starts at 10.30 and ends at 21.30.

You are welcome for a casual game or to just come and watch. It is free and you do not need to register. (The location is a bar, so we ask to buy a drink or a snack to support the venue.)

If you want to play the tournament, you can register on Battlefy using the button below. Registration is 7,50 euro. You need to be present to check in between 10.30 and 11.00. Bring 4 Standard decks of different classes. Format is Conquest Best-of-5 with one ban. (Battlefy requires you to choose 4 classes when registering, but you may change classes at any time before the tournament starts!

This Fireside Tournament is part of the Amsterdam Hearthstone League 2018. All information about the AHL: points, prizes, dates, etc.

See you by the hearth!

Lucas the Innkeeper
