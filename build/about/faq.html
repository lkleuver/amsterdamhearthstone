Frequently asked Questions

(last update: May 31, 2016)

When is the next Amsterdam Hearthstone Fireside Gathering?

We organise a Fireside Gathering one Saturday every month. Check amsterdamhearthstone.nl, Facebook or Meetup for the next date.

What time does it start?

The Fireside location is open from 11 to 22. The tournament is from 11.30 to 21. Tournament check-in closes at 11.30 (no late admissions)

What is the location of the Fireside?

Spelletjescafe 2 Klaveren (De Clercqstraat 136, Amsterdam) is a friendly and cosy pub (bruin café) in Amsterdam-West that offers board and card games and hosts Magic The Gathering tournaments.

Public transport

  • It's very easy to get to the Fireside by public transport.
  • Take tram 13 (from Central Station) or tram 12 (from Station Sloterdijk) to Willem de Zwijgerlaan.
  • Check 9292.nl for times

Parking

  • Parking is available near 2 Klaveren for either €3 (west of the bridge) or €4 (east of the bridge) per hour.
  • A cheaper option is to park at Station Sloterdijk (€1 for the full day), see P+R Transferium Sloterdijk.

Is there an age minimum at your events?

Players of all ages are welcome. You do need written permission from your parents if you are under 16.

What if I get hungry?

No problem, the pub offers dinner, snacks, sandwiches and drinks at very reasonable prices.

Will there be WiFi?

Of course! There is a stable broadband internet connection at the Fireside. Just bring your own laptop, tablet or phone.

I don't speak Dutch. Is that a problem?

No problem. All of our communication is in English. Also, Dutch players don't mind speaking English to you.

What does the Fireside cost?

It's free to come and have a chat, a drink and a friendly game. Tournament registration is €5 per person.

How do I register for the Fireside Gatherings and Tournament?

Right here.

Can I come without playing the tournament?

Of course! You are always welcome for a friendly game and a chat.

I have an idea, suggestion or question

Please share it with us. Contact us through email, Facebook or Meetup.

Is there a (long) break during the Fireside?

No. The tournament is from 11.30 to 21.00. There will be short breaks between every round because we have to wait for every game to finish.

I'm a beginning player. Won't I get eliminated from the tournament right away?

We always play Swiss tournaments without elimination. This means that players of equal skill meet every round and no one is kicked out. The tournament is fun for players of all levels!

Does my Hearthstone account have to be on the EU server?

Yes. You can only join the tournament if your account is on the EU server. Of course, you are welcome to come for a chat and a friendly game if you are on the Americas or Asia servers.

Can I join late?

Late admission is not possible. If you arrive after 11.30, you cannot join the tournament. However, if you know you are going to be late, please contact us before 11 (email, Facebook or Battlefy). We could give you an auto-loss for round 1 and enter you in round 2. This is only possible with explicit persmission from us, and no later than 11.00.

Do I have to prepare for the tournament?

Yes. You must have 3 decks of 3 different classes in your Hearthstone account.

Are there prizes for the tournament?

Yes. We always have first and second place prizes and sometimes more. Prizes could be card packs, Blizzard goodies, gift cards and other fun stuff.
