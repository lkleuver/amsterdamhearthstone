Frequently asked Questions

(last update April 2016)

When is the next Amsterdam Hearthstone Fireside Gathering?

We organise a Fireside Gathering one Saturday every month. Check amsterdamhearthstone.nl, Facebook or Meetup for the next date.

What time does it start?

The Fireside location is open from 11 to 22. The tournament is from 11.30 to 21. Tournament check-in closes at 11.30. (You can't be late!)

What is the location of the Fireside?

Spelletjescafé 2 Klaveren (maps) is a friendly and cosy pub (bruin café) in Amsterdam-West that offers board and card games. The address is De Clercqstraat 136.

Public transport

It's very easy and quick to get to the Fireside by public transport. Check 9292.nl for the fastest route.

Parking at the door

Parking spots are available very near for either €3 (west of the bridge) or €4 (east of the bridge) per hour.

Affordable parking

You can park you car at Station Sloterdijk for only €1 for the whole day, using the special P+R Regeling (website).

What if I get hungry?

No problem, the pub offers dinner, snacks, sandwiches and drinks at very reasonable prices.
Please note that outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the pub. You can bring them, just don't eat or drink them inside.

Is there WiFi?

Of course! There is a stable broadband internet connection at the Fireside. Just bring your own laptop, tablet or phone. We'll take care of the rest.

I don't speak Dutch. Is that a problem?

No problem. All of our communication is in English. We ask Dutch players to speak English whenever it is needed.

What does the Fireside cost?

It's free to come and have a chat, a drink and a friendly game. Tournament registration is €5 per person.

How do I register for the Fireside Gatherings and Tournament?

Right here.

Can I come without playing the tournament?

Of course! You are always welcome for a friendly game and a chat.

I have an idea, suggestion or question

Please share it with us. Contact us through email, Facebook or Meetup.

Is there a (long) break during the Fireside?

We don't take scheduled breaks. However, there will be short breaks between every round while waiting for the last games to finish.

I'm a beginning player. Won't I get eliminated from the tournament right away?

We always play Swiss tournaments without elimination. This means that players of equal skill meet every round and no one is kicked out. The tournament is fun for players of all levels!

Does my Hearthstone account have to be on the EU server?

Yes. You can only join the tournament if your account is on the EU server. Of course, you are welcome to come for a chat and a friendly game if you are on a differenct server.

Can I join late?

Late admission is not possible. When we start the tournament at 11.30 sharp, there is no way to add new players. However, if you know you are going to be late and you contact us no later than 11.00, we could start you in round two, with an auto-loss in round one. This is only possible with explicit permission, given before 11.00.

Do I have to prepare for the tournament?

Yes. You must have 3 decks of 3 different classes in your Hearthstone account.

Are there prizes for the tournament?

Yes. We always have first and second place prizes and sometimes more. Prizes could be card packs, Blizzard goodies, gift cards and other fun stuff.
